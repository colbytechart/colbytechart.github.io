Lunar Lion Scientific Visualization

In 2014, I volunteered scientific visualization work for Penn State University's Lunar Lion Team to aid their education/outreach efforts during the Google Lunar XPrize Competition. I rebuilt a production-ready model of the lander from the team's CAD files, textured, lit, rendered, and animated the above sequence in close collaboration with the team lead while referencing the team's construction and mission specs.

Karamba Security Ad

For this Karamba Security ad, I was asked to design and iterate concepts for a fictitious computer operating system. Once a design was approved, I created a functional template that could be modified for individual monitors on-set. I designed and animated every screen element in the ad. Most of the screens were shot practically, but a small number needed to be composited in post-production. After principal production, I was tasked with a handful of roto-paint shots and screen comps.
